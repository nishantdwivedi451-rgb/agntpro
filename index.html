The story behind AgntPro.ai

After 7+ years deploying WhatsApp, RCS, voice, and website chatbot solutions for top banks, retailers, and electronics giants, I saw one problem everywhere.

Businesses spend months building conversation flows with drag-and-drop tools. By the time they go live, the use case has changed.

I have built more than 100 AI-powered conversational agents for India's largest companies spanning BFSI, retail, and consumer electronics.

The bottleneck is not technology — it is the workflow. Businesses need a way to describe what they want in plain English and deploy across every channel.
