The story behind AgntPro.ai

After spending 7+ years in the trenches of enterprise messaging — deploying WhatsApp Business API, RCS, and voice solutions for India's top banks, leading retail chains, and electronics giants — I saw the same problem everywhere:

Businesses spend months building conversation flows with drag-and-drop tools. By the time they go live, the use case has changed.

I've personally built and deployed more than 100 AI-powered conversational agents for some of India's largest companies — spanning BFSI, retail, and consumer electronics. These include top private sector banks, leading retail chains, and household electronics brands that millions of Indians interact with daily.

That experience taught me one thing: the bottleneck isn't the technology — it's the workflow. Flowchart-based builders are too rigid. Code-based solutions need engineering teams. What businesses actually need is a way to describe what they want in plain English and have an intelligent agent figure out the rest.

That's exactly what AgntPro.ai does.
